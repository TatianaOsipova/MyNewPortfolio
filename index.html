    Landing Page

    A single, focused webpage designed to capture leads or promote a product, service, or campaign. Landing pages usually have a specific goal, such as collecting contact information, encouraging sign-ups, or leading users to make a purchase.

    Multi-Page Websites

    These are websites with multiple pages that provide a deeper structure for complex content. Ideal for businesses, organizations, or services that need to present detailed information across various sections (e.g., "About Us," "Services," "Products," "Contact").

    One-Page Websites

    Unlike a landing page, this is a fully functional website on a single page that uses sections for different types of information (About, Services, Contact) rather than separate pages. It's ideal for small businesses or individuals who want a minimalist, fast, and focused website.

    Portfolio Website

    Showcases personal or business work (photographers, artists, designers). Often visually oriented, with gallery features.

    Blog Website

    Dedicated to publishing regular posts or articles. A blog can be part of a larger multi-page site or standalone.

    E-Commerce Websites

    These sites are designed to sell products or services online. They can range from a small catalog to a massive store.
